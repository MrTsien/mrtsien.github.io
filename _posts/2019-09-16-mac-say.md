# 让 Mac 命令行说话

##  复制下面的命令，在终端中运行即可。
```
say --voice="Sin-ji" 您好，我叫 Sin-ji 。我講廣東話。
```

- 不同的声音
```
say -v Sin-ji  "Hello, I am Chinese.  What do you want to eat?"
say -v Thomas  "Hello, I am French.  What do you want to eat?"
say -v Carmit  "Hello, I am  Israeli.  What do you want to eat?"
```
